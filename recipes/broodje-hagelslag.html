Broodje Hagelslag

The finished endresult: a simple but tasty meal

Very easy to make. Let it rain hagelslag!

Not very healthy but it is very delicious yum!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Hagelslag
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take a piece of bread and put butter on it
  2. Sprinkle on the hagelslag
  3. Enjoy!
