Rice Paper Omelette

The finished endresult: fluffy but crunchy streetfood dish

Very easy to make, super tasty.

Also very healthy as well. Can be included in a keto diet!

Ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Eggs
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Put the rice paper on a heated pan
  2. Crack the eggs on top
  3. Put mayo on it and fold it in half
